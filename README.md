> **Trade Less. Trade Better.**

Heavenly Edge AI is a professional TradingView indicator built for disciplined,
rule-based trading.

Instead of simply generating Buy and Sell signals, Heavenly Edge AI acts as a
trading assistant by analyzing market structure, liquidity, order flow, timing,
and risk before approving a trade.

---

# Vision

Build a trading operating system that helps traders:

• Take fewer, higher-quality trades
• Avoid emotional decisions
• Understand why a setup is valid
• Manage trades professionally
• Improve consistency over time

---

# Primary Market

MNQ Futures

Default Timeframe

1 Minute

---

# Core Philosophy

Every signal must answer three questions.

1. Why now?

2. Why here?

3. Why not earlier?

If those questions cannot be answered, there is no trade.
